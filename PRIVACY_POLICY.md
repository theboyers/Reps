# Privacy Policy

**App:** Reps  
**Developer:** Christopher Boyer  
**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

## The short version

Reps collects nothing. It stores nothing outside your device. It contacts no servers.

## Data collection

Reps does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or any other information. There are no third-party SDKs, advertising libraries, or tracking tools included in the app.

## Data storage

The only data the app writes is the workout routines you create yourself. This data is stored exclusively in your device's local SharedPreferences storage and remains entirely under your control. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Export feature to create a file, which is saved to a location you choose.

## Permissions

Reps requests no Android permissions.

## Backups

If you have Android's Auto Backup enabled, your routine library may be backed up to your personal Google Drive account as part of Android's standard device backup. This is controlled entirely by your Android settings and Google account — the app does not initiate or control this process.

## Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes it will be updated in this file with a new date at the top.
